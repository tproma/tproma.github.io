About Me

I am a Research Associate in the Agency Lab at Independent University, Bangadesh. I have completed my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB). I have worked in the domain of Computer Vision and Machine Learning of CVC. At CVC, i have worked on Medicine recognition using intrinsic geometric properties, Vision based trajectory follower with swarm robotics, autonomous navigation of rover. Currently, i am working on the feature based pill image recognition using Deep learning for the National Database of Pill Image, Bangladesh (NDPIB). My research interest lies on Digital Image processing, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and Robotics.
