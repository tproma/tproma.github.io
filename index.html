About Me

I have completed my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB). I have worked in the domain of Computer Vision and Machine Learning. At CVC, i have worked on Medicine recognition using intrinsic geometric properties, Vision based trajectory follower with swarm robotics, autonomous navigation of rover. I have also worked on the feature based pill image recognition using Deep learning for the National Database of Pill Image, Bangladesh (NDPIB). Currently, i am working in Agency lab , IUB as an research Associate. My research interest lies on Digital Image processing, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and Robotics.
