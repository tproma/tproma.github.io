About Me

I am a Research Associate in the Agency Lab at Independent University, Bangadesh. I have completed my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB). Previously, I have worked in the domain of Computer Vision and Machine Learning at Computer Vision and Cybernetics Research Group (CVC) . At CVC, i have worked on Medicine recognition using intrinsic geometric properties, Vision based trajectory follower with swarm robotics, autonomous navigation of rover and Image detection system of rover. Currently, i am working on the feature based pill image recognition using Deep learning for the National Database of Pill Image, Bangladesh (NDPIB).
